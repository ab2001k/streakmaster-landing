Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2026

Thank you for choosing StreakMaster Pro ("we", "us", or "our"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

We believe in privacy first. StreakMaster Pro is designed to store the majority of your data locally on your device.

  • User Profile: Your display name and profile picture are stored locally and synced securely to Supabase for leaderboard functionality if you choose to sign up.
  • Habit Data: Your streaks, goals, and history are stored locally on your device and backed up to Supabase to prevent data loss.
  • Device Information: We may collect basic device information (model, OS version) for crash reporting and optimization.

2. Permissions

To provide full functionality, the app requires the following permissions:

  • Notifications & Alarms: Used strictly to send you the reminders you set for your habits.
  • Storage (Read/Write): Used only for the "Backup & Restore" feature to save your JSON data file to your device.

3. Data Security

Your data is yours. We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal identification information to others. Account authentication and database services are provided by Supabase, a secure industry-standard backend.

4. Third-Party Services

The app may contain links to third-party websites (e.g., GitHub). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external sites.

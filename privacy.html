Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 2026

At StreakMaster Pro, we believe your habits are your business. Our commitment is to provide a powerful productivity tool while keeping your personal data under your control. This policy outlines our transparent approach to data handling.

1. Information Governance

We prioritize local-first storage. Most of your journey stays on your device.

  • User Identity: If you choose to use our Cloud Sync/Leaderboard, your display name and profile image are securely synced via Supabase.
  • Progress Data: Your streaks, history, and goals are backed up to our encrypted cloud database to ensure you never lose your progress.
  • Analytics: Anonymous technical data (OS version, crash logs) may be collected to improve performance.

2. System Permissions

StreakMaster Pro requests only the minimum necessary permissions to function effectively:

  • Notifications: To trigger the habit reminders you specifically schedule.
  • File Access: Strictly for the "Manual Backup" feature, allowing you to export your data as a portable JSON file.

3. Data Integrity & Sharing

We do not sell your data. Period. We use Supabase, an industry-leading backend service, to provide enterprise-grade encryption for your synced data. We do not track your physical location or access your private contacts.
